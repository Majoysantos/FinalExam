Pharsa

Curse

Pharsa casts a spell on a target area, dealing 300 / 325 / 350 / 375 / 400 / 425 (+120% Total Magic Power) Magic Damage to enemies within the area of effect and marking them for 4 seconds.

Energy Impact

Pharsa releases a wave of magic energy, dealing 425 / 470 / 515 / 560 / 605 / 650 (+145% Total Magic Power) Magic Damage to all enemies caught in its path.

Air Strike

Pharsa flies into mid-air and launches air raids on the target area for up to 4 rounds within the next 8 seconds, with each round dealing 620 / 770 / 920 (+160% Total Magic Power) Magic Damage to the enemy hit.
